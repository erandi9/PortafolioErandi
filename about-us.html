Hello! I'm Erandi



I am a Mexican designer, currently living in Cd. Juárez, near the border with El Paso, Tx.

I graduated as a Product Designer, a year later after my graduation I found my passion for the User Experience and I fell in love with it, so, here I am! Now with multiple certifications in: UX design, HTML5, CSS3 and GitHub pages.

I am very creative and highly artistic, I enjoy learning new things. When I start a new project the first thing I do is analyze and try to empathize with the customer, not the client, the customer, I try to change the "who" for the "why" of what I am trying to do.
