About the project

Working as the only designer of the project, I design and prototyped the first version of the platform. I created the product structure and refined several versions of it. My main target is everyone who is getting married and what to have something different from paper invitations and wedding planners who can use the platform as an organizer. From the platform you will be able to control the guest list, sitting charts, food allergies, and much more.


Process

My first step as User Experience was searching the competition. After analyzing what other companies do, I carry out some surveys and interviews to get to know better the user, then we defined the personas make and ideation scenarios and jumped right into the ideation phase. From low fidelity sketches to a high-fidelity prototype, we gradually created the first version of the platform.

We sent out a survey and conducted interviews to learn how people prefer their wedding invitations. Most of the people didn’t know how the hole wedding platform works and how can it be replaced for a traditional wedding invitation, when we explain the hole process and the things that they can manage without so much effort, they were very interested.
